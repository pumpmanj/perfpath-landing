Tools Built Specifically for Perfusionists
The Problem

Every perfusionist faces the same
fragmented, manual documentation problem

Shadow hours logged on paper. Clinical cases tracked in spreadsheets. CEUs scattered across emails. Hospital research done through word of mouth. The perfusion profession is one of the most demanding in medicine — and it has been operating without the tools it deserves.

The Platform

Four pillars. One path.

Pillar 01
◈

Shadow Tracker

For pre-applicants building their application. Log every shadowing experience, track hours, and generate a clean PDF for program submissions.

  • Shadowing hour log
  • Program requirement tracker
  • CCP finder map
  • Auto-generated shadow PDF
Core Feature
Pillar 02
◉

Case Logger

For students in accredited programs. ABCP-compliant case logging with subcategory tracking, CCP number verification, and one-tap PDF export.

  • 75-case progress tracker
  • Pediatric & ECMO subcategories
  • Instructor CCP verification
  • ABCP-format PDF export
Pillar 03
◆

Hospital Directory

For perfusionists at every stage. A crowdsourced database of hospitals — equipment, case mix, call structure, city life, and hiring status.

  • Pump & equipment by hospital
  • Call structure & case volume
  • City and lifestyle overview
  • Hiring status & job leads
Coming Later
Pillar 04
⬡

Community Hub

A dedicated space built around the perfusion profession. Ask clinical questions, share case experiences, and connect with perfusionists across the country — all within the same platform where you track your career.

  • Peer case consultation
  • Protocol & priming library
  • Anonymized salary database
  • CEU tracking & career tools
